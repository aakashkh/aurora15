Bits n Chits

A Collage is an artwork made from an assemblage of different forms, thus creating a new whole. It includes newspapers, magazine clippings, paint, crumbled papers or photographs glued to a canvas or paper. Collage Making took a dramatic transmutation in 20th century as an art of novelty. Tune your ideas and make magic happen on the themes given.



Mehandi
