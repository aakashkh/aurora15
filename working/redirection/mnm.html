Rules

1. The forms for the first round will be provided by the organisers on the first day of Aurora`15.
2. The final decision for the GD/JAM round in MnM rests with the organising team and jury.
3. The notifications and results of each round will be relayed to the participants as soon as possible.
4. Misbehaviour, use of foul language or any form of cheating during any round is prohibited.
5. All participants are requested to be punctual for each round. Any sort of delay will not be entertained and may lead to disqualification.



Timeline

Format of the Event:

1st Stage:
Form filling by participants. Among all these forms, the best ones will be selected. So don your writing hats so that your form can truly convince us to get you selected for the next round. Be true to what you write in the form.

To Participate - Click Here

2nd Stage:
The participants selected from all the forms will take part in Group Discussion (GD) or Jest a Minute (JAM). Simple enough if you are good at expressing your views in front of people.
3rd Stage:
Participants selected from the GD will have personal interview (PI). Now this is the stage where you really have to convince us with your skills and get your ticket to the main event of MnM.

Events that will be held during the 3 days of Aurora:

Day 1: It will cover all the events mentioned above.
Day 2: The task round - Mainly there would be around 2-3 tasks in groups and some individual tasks to test you physically and mentally. Only the tough ones will last, for others its goodbye.
Day 3: The Finals - The final round is a mystery that will be unveiled to those who have the grit to conquer the previous rounds and become a finalist.
