Desi Jugaad

Desi jugaad would test how much profess you have as a jugaadu and how much you can spontaneously arrange things.You have to find a number of items which are listed in a fixed amount of time. The one who completes our checklist in the least amount of time takes away the hamper.

In this fun-filled event you have to balance a balloon within a group of three and carry it till the other side without dropping it down. The one who collects the maximum balloons at the end shall win.
